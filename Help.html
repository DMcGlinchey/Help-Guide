Logging on

Once you web browser and enter: www.end2endonline.com
Enter your Waitrose unique: Username and Password
Click Log On.

Finding your way around

You will now see a page similar to this containing Waitrose POS that is available to order.
To order POS, click on the 'Order POS' tab in the top left hand corner.
Select on Area of Store from the dropdown menu (see left).
You will now be taken to all POS that is available to order in that category.
The Help Desk number is in the top right hand corner for any problems with ordering POS.
Please note this is not a technical support help desk






Finding your Products

Once you have chosen your POS category you will see all available POS in that category displayed below with a thumbnail.
You can also search for POS within each category by using the search panels located next to the category menu. These search panels allow you to search by either Code or Description.
If you entereted a code or a description, press enter or click on the search icon.
Click here to clear the search.
Be aware that some categories do have several pages of POS that is available to order. To view the different pages of POS, click on the next page icon located in the top right hand corner of the page. If you would like to view more than the default 20 items per page, click on the dropdown menu and select your desired amount.




Understanding the icons

Once you have found POS, you can click on the thumbnail or the preview icon which will give you the option to view on screen before you order.
Click here to add your chosen amount to your shopping basket.
Click here to view a preview of the POS.

Order your Product

Once you have found POS and you have entered the amount that you would like to order. Click on the shopping basket icon to add the POS to your Basket which will appear on the right-hand side of the page.
The total price will update as you add POS to your basket. Click on the blue arrow to expand the basket to show you what you are purchasing.
If you wish to remove anything from your basket at any time, click on the red cross.
Feel free to continue to add items to your basket.
Once you have finished, click Go To Checkout.

Checkout

The checkout contains all your selected items
If an item you require is out of stock a back order will be requested. This will display a warning and highlight affected items in red.
Confirm your order is correct, however if you wish to remove anything from your order, click on the red cross, your basket will now update displaying the removing items. If you remove all items from your shopping basket you will be given the option to return to the main shopping menu.
Before you place your order, select a Reason For Order from the dropdown menu.
Type your name into the Employee Name box.
Select Delivery Date. Urgent delivery will incur a charge, these requests will go to the Head Office for authorisation.
Click Place Order and your order will be Processed

Log Off

Once you have finished ordering POS, click Log Off.
You will now be returned to the End2End login page

It is important that you remember to log Off once you have finished using the End2End software. This is to ensure POS is not accidently ordered under your username by other members of staff.
